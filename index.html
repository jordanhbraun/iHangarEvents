About Innovation Hangar

Located in the iconic Palace of Fine Arts, the Innovation Hangar is designed to be an epicenter of learning and exploration. The mission is to provide a physical space where thinkers, doers and critics can come together under one roof.

Innovation Hangar is a 501 (c)(3) non-profit organization. The Palace of Fine Arts is owned and managed by the San Francisco Recreation and Parks department

What's Inside?

Visit the hangar to see the iconic building, celebrating its 100 year anniversary, and was first built for the 1915 Panama – Pacific International Exposition. Sponsored by the California Historical Society.

The hangar is also home to organizations including: Maker Media, Wearable World, Imagination Playground, DSF Clothing Co, Spark Lab, and Palace Games
